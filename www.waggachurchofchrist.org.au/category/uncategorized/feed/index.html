]]> It’s that time of year again!

That’s right, time for the biggest Garage Sale in the southern hemisphere! Well okay, the record books may not be confirmed….. but one things for sure, it guarantees to once again be the biggest Garage Sale our region has ever seen!

Each year the Wagga Wagga Church Community gets together to host a massive one day Garage Sale at the Wagga Wagga Baptist Church on Tarcutta St. Funds raised go towards supporting the Wagga Inter-Church Christian Education Team (WICCET) to provide scripture into our schools.

There is always plenty of  hands required with a lot of hard work needed leading up to the big day, and also on the actual day and for clean up – so if you can help in one way or another, please contact Janine Menzies on 0409 607 657
