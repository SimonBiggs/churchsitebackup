]]> Have a read of this great verse

 Rejoice always,  pray continually,  give thanks in all circumstances;for this is God’s will for you in Christ Jesus. 1 Thes 5:16-18

It is clear from this verse and others like it that we are meant to be a people of hope. Hope should fill us because of the gospel itself. We have become the children of God Himself. We were a people who should have been condemned to death because of our own sin. Despite this, God has chosen to pay for our sins himself and redeem us to live with Him forever. So there is plenty to rejoice in.  There is plenty to be thankful for. However, there is also plenty to be praying about. Why else would God advise us to pray continually. A people of eternal life still need to pray. We are caught in this sinful world and in our weak bodies. We can be prone to temptations of all sorts if we do not pray and seek God;s help. The new testament church was promised the Holy Spirit. Pray regularly and make life a continual Communion with God. In praying regularly like this you will avoid a great many mistakes and open yourself up to the favour of God which is worth far more than anything you could work for, and you can not buy God’s favour either.  You have to please Him. So Pray.

]]> In many ways the message of the bible that God gives us is a message of hope. We begin with creation and almost immediately man rebels by disobeying the most simple and may I add Only rule they have,i.e don’t eat from the tree in the centre of the garden. The problem is that there is no sin in God and he can not be united with a sinful people. So this means separation from God Himself. Since God is there source of life, this also means death. People now seemed to have no hope. However, the rest of the bible sets about showing how God did not abandon His creation. Instead He works with and teaches us from a distance until at the great climax of hope He sends His own son Jesus to pay for our sin so that we once again have the opportunity to return to eternal life in God. Some years ago Mars Hill church released the following video that you view yourself which goes through the main events of the bible. They called it The Hope. It’s a short movie length, but when you have the time have a look.  (URL -https://vimeo.com/44835542)
